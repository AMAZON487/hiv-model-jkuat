R₀ Derivation (Next Generation Matrix)
Using van den Driessche & Watmough (2002) — spectral radius of F·V⁻¹
R₀ = (β × k) / [(μ + k)(μ + δ + τ)]
R₀ = (1.82 × 0.1095) / [(0.014 + 0.1095)(0.014 + 0.025 + 0.152)]
